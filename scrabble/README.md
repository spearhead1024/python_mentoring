# Scrabble Helper

Write a program called `scrabble.py` that will find all the possible words you can make from a set of Scrabble tiles.

* Blank/Wild - 2 tiles
* A – 9 tiles
* B – 2 tiles
* C – 2 tiles
* D – 4 tiles
* E - 12 tiles
* F - 2 tiles
* G - 3 tiles
* H - 2 tiles
* I - 9 tiles
* J - 1 tile
* K - 1 tile
* L - 4 tiles
* M - 2 tiles
* N - 6 tiles
* O - 8 tiles
* P - 2 tiles
* Q - 1 tile
* R - 6 tiles
* S - 4 tiles
* T - 6 tiles
* U - 4 tiles
* V - 2 tiles
* W - 2 tiles
* X - 1 tile
* Y - 2 tiles
* Z - 1 tile
